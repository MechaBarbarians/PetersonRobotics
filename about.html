About Us

Our Origin

Founded in 2024 by Naethaniel “Nate” Peterson, we began by prototyping a 3D-printed shoreline barrier in a Salt Lake City workshop. This success sparked the creation of Project Evo: a cohesive suite of modular robots.

Mission & Values

We combine renewable energy, open-source design, and sustainable materials to build adaptable robotic platforms. Our core values—Integrity, Sustainability, Inclusivity, Adaptability, Innovation—guide every project.

Community Engagement

We host annual workshops, “Bot Beach Day,” and collaborate with universities and NGOs to validate our systems in real-world conditions, from deserts to coral reefs.
