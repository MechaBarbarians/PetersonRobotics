Harsh-Terrain Builder Robot

This modular arm system accepts drill, welder, and extruder toolheads, constructing shelters from in-situ resources such as regolith, gravel, or soil mixed with binders.

Goals

  • Autonomously assemble a 3x3m shelter within 12 hours.
  • Validate foldable printer modules for compact transport.
  • Integrate wireless power and remote command capabilities.
  • Demonstrate self-assembly on uneven, unprepared ground.
