Indigo Sea Vessel

Indigo is a 6m solar-wind hybrid catamaran rated to 100m depth operations. Equipped with multibeam sonar and hydrophones, it performs bathymetric surveys while acting as a relay for submerged modules.

Goals

  • Conduct high-resolution seafloor mapping with <1m resolution.
  • Sustain 14-day autonomous deployments using renewable energy.
  • Launch and recover underwater submodules for targeted inspections.
  • Provide a mobile command center and data hub at sea.
