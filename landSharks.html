Land Sharks Amphibious Crawler

Land Sharks autonomously harvest local aggregates—sand, shell, minerals—and process them into a rapid-set cement. A precision 3D-print extruder then constructs shoreline barriers or temporary structures.

Goals

  • Print continuous barrier walls up to 1m height directly on-site.
  • Utilize 80% in-situ materials to reduce supply logistics.
  • Complete barrier construction at 0.5 m³/hour throughput.
  • Test phased mobility upgrades for complex terrain navigation.
