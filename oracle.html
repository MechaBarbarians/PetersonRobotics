Oracle Power & Logistics Hub

Oracle features a 10 kWh battery bank, wireless charging pads, and dock interfaces. Its heavy-duty chassis hauls up to 100 kg of supplies or sample loads, enabling continuous operation of adjacent modules.

Goals

  • Enable 48-hour continuous field operations via dynamic power routing.
  • Transport tools, batteries, and collected samples across distances up to 5 km.
  • Interface with solar arrays and wind turbines for recharging.
  • Facilitate mesh-network data relays between modules.
