SpyderV1 Recon Rover

SpyderV1 boasts six high-torque servos powering articulated legs, enabling traversal of rocky, sandy, and forested terrains. Integrated RGB, depth, and thermal sensors feed into an onboard Jetson Nano running ROS for autonomous mapping and obstacle avoidance.

Goals

  • Achieve sub-10 cm mapping accuracy in unstructured environments.
  • Stream real-time multi-modal sensor data to remote operators.
  • Support modular payloads (LiDAR, chemical sensors) for diverse missions.
  • Maintain >8 hours of battery life under continuous operation.
