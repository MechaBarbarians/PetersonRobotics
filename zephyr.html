Zephyr Aerial Delivery Drone

Zephyr is our high-endurance aerial delivery drone built to transport critical supplies across challenging terrain. With a hybrid electric/solar powertrain, Zephyr achieves extended flight times for remote operations.

Functionality

  • Payload Capacity: Up to 5 kg of medical kits, tools, or samples.
  • Flight Range: 20 km autonomous mission profile with GPS waypoint navigation.
  • Endurance: Up to 2 hours on battery, extended with solar recharging panels.
  • Sensors: Integrated GPS, LiDAR obstacle avoidance, RGB camera for precision delivery.
  • Communication: Secure mesh-network link for real-time telemetry and control beyond line of sight.

Goals

  • Demonstrate reliable medical supply drops in remote locations.
  • Achieve semi-autonomous operation under dynamic environmental conditions.
  • Integrate with ground logistics platform (Oracle) for coordinated delivery.
  • Validate mid-air docking and battery swap for continuous operations.
